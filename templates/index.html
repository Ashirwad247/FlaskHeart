Welcome to the Heart Disease Prediction App

This application helps you predict the likelihood of heart disease based on various health parameters. By entering your health data, we will analyze the results and give you a prediction on whether you are at risk for heart disease. This tool is built using machine learning and aims to assist individuals in understanding their heart health better.

About This Application

Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death worldwide. This application uses a machine learning model to predict the risk of heart disease based on common health parameters such as age, cholesterol levels, blood pressure, and more. By analyzing these inputs, the app can help you assess whether you are at risk for heart disease and encourage you to seek professional medical advice if necessary.

The model was trained using real-world data and provides an easy-to-use interface to help individuals take control of their health. Remember, this is a predictive tool and not a substitute for professional medical diagnosis.
